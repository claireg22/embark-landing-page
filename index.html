What is embark?

Embark is a student-based community focused on helping students develop professional skills that prepare them to get and succeed in their next opportunity!

For Students:

  • Ask career-focused questions on a filterable forum
  • Get reliable answers, advice, and resources from
    clubs at your university
  • Discover new clubs and events at your university

For Clubs:

  • Reach and recruit new students
  • Share resources and create events all on one platform
  • Collaborate with other clubs to host larger events
